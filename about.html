Founded

Walter Zainey started the Greenwood Furnace Co. in the Greenwood neighborhood of Seattle in 1918 and quickly built a reputation for providing quality products and services.

Transition

Pat Martin purchased the company. Over the next 7 years, Greenwood Heating and Home Services become the 46th fastest growing company in Washington.

Lennox

Ms. Martin sold Greenwood Heating to Service Experts. Service Experts is a national chain of service companies with over 200 locations and controlled by Lennox International.
